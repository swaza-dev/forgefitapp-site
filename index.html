Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ForgeFit

What is ForgeFit? +
ForgeFit is a comprehensive fitness tracking app that monitors all 4 pillars of fitness: Training, Nutrition, Hydration, and Sleep. With AI coaching in 11 languages, 52-week adaptive programs, and 5,700+ exercises, it's your complete fitness system in one app. Learn more about features.
How much does ForgeFit cost? +
ForgeFit costs $3.99/month or $19.99/year (save 58%). We offer a 7-day free trial so you can try all features risk-free. That's less than one coffee per month for your complete fitness system. Start your free trial.
What platforms are supported? +
ForgeFit is available on both iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android devices. Download from the App Store or Google Play.
Do I need an internet connection? +
No! ForgeFit works completely offline for workout logging, progress tracking, and viewing your programs. You only need internet for AI coach conversations and syncing data across devices.
How does the AI coaching work? +
Your AI coach (Coach Alex) understands your training program, progress, and goals. Ask questions about training, nutrition, supplements, or form checks in natural conversation. Coach Alex speaks 11 languages and understands cultural context and local foods. See AI coaching in action.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes! Cancel anytime through your App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) subscription settings. No cancellation fees. Your data remains accessible until your subscription period ends.
Is my data private and secure? +
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your personal information. Health data integrations (Apple Health, Google Health Connect) follow strict privacy standards. Read our full Privacy Policy.
Will I get Coach Alex or Coach Rachel? +
You'll be paired with the coach that best matches your profile. Coach Alex specializes in male physiology, while Coach Rachel specializes in female physiology and pregnancy fitness. Both have the same expertise, speak 11 languages, and understand cultural context and local foods.
What languages are supported? +
ForgeFit supports 11 languages: English, Spanish, Portuguese, German, French, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Russian, and Turkish. Your AI coach understands cultural context and local foods in each language.
